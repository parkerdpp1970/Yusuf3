Case Study

Yusuf Ozturk

Age:41 years old
Occupation:Computer Programmer / Manager
Location:Istanbul, Turkey
Family:Married, Father of three

Background

At 41, Yusuf is looking to regain the fitness he once had as an accomplished long-distance runner. His career and family life have taken priority for the last decade, leading to a significant decrease in regular physical activity. Having risen from 70kg to 80kg, he is ready to reconnect with his athletic identity.

Dietary Habits & Lifestyle

Current Eating Patterns:

  • Most meals are home-cooked and generally balanced.
  • Mediterranean-style influence but with a tendency towards large portions.
  • Weekend eating can be excessive compared to the work week.

Physical Activity:

  • Dislikes gyms and weight training; prefers the outdoors.
  • Active as a father, but seeks personal athletic fulfilment.
  • Committed to a routine of 3-4 sessions per week (1 hour each).

Challenges:

  • Long working hours due to responsibilities as a manager and lead programmer.
  • Dealing with team management and technical demands creates a hectic work schedule.
  • High-pressure environment makes regular meal timing and consistent training sessions difficult to maintain.

Health & Wellness

Height:1.76 m
Current Weight:80 kg
Physical Activity:Outdoor Running
Stress Level:High
Goal Weight:70 kg
Main Goal: Yusuf wants to lose 10 kg and improve his cardiovascular fitness so he can run comfortably with his active children and eventually complete a half marathon.
